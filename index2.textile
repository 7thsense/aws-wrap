h3. Non-blocking Amazon Web Service Library

For Scala and Java. We support the following services:

 * SimpleDB
 * DynamoDB
 * S3
 * Simple Notification Service (SNS)
 * Simple Queue Service (SQS)
 * Simple Email Service (SES)

h3. Use in SBT or Play

bc. "aws" %% "dynamodb" % "0.1-SNAPSHOT"

If you're in a Play 2.1 project, make sure to exclude Play from the dependencies:

bc. "aws" %% "dynamodb" % "0.1-SNAPSHOT" exclude("play", "play_2.10")

h3. Use in Maven

bc. <dependency>
	<groupId>aws</groupId>
	<artifactId>dynamodb</artifactId>
	<version>0.1-SNAPSHOT</version>
</dependency>
